Privacy Policy – Tracklyst

Last Updated: February 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Tracklyst (the “App”) collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use the App.

Owner: Muhammad Khan
Contact: siliconbeachstudio@outlook.com

If you do not agree with this Privacy Policy, please do not use the App.

1) Summary

Your study data is stored locally on your device. Subscriptions are processed by Apple through the App Store. We use RevenueCat to manage subscription status and entitlements. We do not sell your personal data. You can export your data as a JSON file and control where it is stored or shared. We do not use your data for tracking across apps and websites.

2) Information We Collect

2.1 Data You Create or Enter (Stored Locally)

The App may store information locally on your device, such as:

  • study sessions
  • daily/weekly goals
  • preferences and settings
  • backup/export data (when you export)

2.2 Notifications Data

If enabled, the App schedules local notifications and may store:

  • reminder settings (such as reminder time)
  • internal identifiers used to manage scheduled notifications

2.3 Subscription and Entitlement Data

Payments are processed by Apple. We use RevenueCat to determine subscription status and unlock Pro features.

RevenueCat may process information such as:

  • subscription status and entitlement status
  • product identifiers (monthly/yearly)
  • receipt validation data
  • anonymous user identifiers used to manage entitlements and restores

2.4 Technical Information

We may process limited technical information for functionality and stability, such as:

  • device type
  • operating system version
  • app version
  • language/region

We do not use this information for advertising or analytics.

3) How We Use Information

We use the information described above to:

  • provide core features of the App
  • track study sessions and goals
  • manage notifications (if enabled)
  • process subscriptions and unlock Pro features
  • restore purchases
  • improve performance and reliability
  • respond to support requests

4) How We Share Information

We may share limited information with the following parties to provide the App:

Apple

Apple processes subscription payments and provides purchase validation through the App Store.

RevenueCat

RevenueCat manages subscription entitlements and may process purchase receipts and subscription status information.

File Sharing Services (Export)

If you export data and share/save it (e.g., Files, iCloud, email, messaging apps), the exported file is handled by the services you choose. You control where exported data is stored or shared.

We do not sell your personal data.

5) Data Storage, Security, and Retention

  • Most data is stored locally on your device.
  • Data remains on your device until you delete it, clear app storage, or uninstall the App.
  • Subscription records may be retained by Apple and RevenueCat according to their policies.
  • We use reasonable security practices, but no system is 100% secure.

6) Your Choices and Controls

You can:

  • enable or disable notifications
  • manage or cancel subscriptions via Apple ID subscription settings
  • restore purchases
  • export and control your data
  • delete the App to remove locally stored data

7) Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8) International Users

Third-party services such as Apple and RevenueCat may process information on servers located in different countries. By using the App, you acknowledge that your information may be processed outside your country.

9) Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will update the “Last Updated” date. Continued use of the App means acceptance of changes.
