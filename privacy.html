Privacy Policy – Tracklyst

Last Updated: February 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Tracklyst (the “App”) collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use the App.

Owner: Muhammad Khan
Contact: siliconbeachstudio@outlook.com

If you do not agree with this Privacy Policy, please do not use the App.

1) Summary

  • Your study data is stored locally on your device.
  • Subscriptions are processed by Apple through the App Store.
  • We use RevenueCat to manage subscription status and entitlements.
  • We do not sell your personal data.
  • You can export your data as a JSON file and control where it is stored or shared.

2) Information We Collect

2.1 Data You Create or Enter (Stored Locally)

The App may store study sessions, goals, preferences, and backup/export data locally on your device.

2.2 Notifications Data

If enabled, the App schedules local notifications and may store reminder settings and internal identifiers.

2.3 Subscription and Entitlement Data

Payments are processed by Apple. We use RevenueCat to determine subscription status and unlock Pro features.

RevenueCat may process subscription status, product identifiers, receipt validation data, and anonymous user identifiers.

2.4 Technical Information

We may collect limited technical information such as device type, OS version, app version, and language for functionality and stability.

3) How We Use Information

  • Provide core features of the App
  • Track study sessions and goals
  • Manage notifications
  • Process subscriptions
  • Restore purchases
  • Improve performance and reliability
  • Respond to support requests

4) How We Share Information

Apple

Processes subscription payments and provides purchase validation.

RevenueCat

Manages subscription entitlements and processes purchase receipts.

File Sharing

Exported files are handled by services you choose (Files, iCloud, email, etc.). You control where exported data is stored.

We do not sell personal data.

5) Data Storage, Security, and Retention

  • Most data is stored locally on your device.
  • Data remains until deleted or the app is uninstalled.
  • Subscription records are retained by Apple and RevenueCat.
  • We use reasonable security practices but no system is 100% secure.

6) Your Choices and Controls

  • Enable or disable notifications
  • Manage or cancel subscriptions via Apple ID
  • Restore purchases
  • Export and control your data
  • Delete the App to remove local data

7) Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 (or minimum age required in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8) International Users

Third-party services such as Apple and RevenueCat may process information on servers located in different countries. By using the App, you acknowledge that your information may be processed outside your country.

9) Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will update the “Last Updated” date. Continued use of the App means acceptance of changes.
